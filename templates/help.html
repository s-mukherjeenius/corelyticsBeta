Pro Tips
  • Use specific descriptions when logging meals (e.g., "Grilled chicken breast 200g" instead of just "Chicken").
  • Update your current weight in Settings weekly to keep your BMR accurate.
  • Enable "Continuous Listening" in the Logic chat for a hands-free experience while cooking!
